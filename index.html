TASM Visitor Portal

In partnership with the Tulsa Air and Space Museum and my own team of four, we built a digital visitor portal to replace the paper sign-in sheets they had been using. The frontend was designed in Webflow, and the backend was built with Firebase and Node.js. We built a data dashboard and visitor map using Python with Dash and Plotly. The application is designed to both make it easier for new and returning visitors to sign in, and to provide the museum with a way to track visitor data and trends, assisting them with grant applications and other funding opportunities.
